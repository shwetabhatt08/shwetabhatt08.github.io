I'm an independent AI advisor and an experience Applied Scientist.I have 10+ years of professional experience in AI and computing and have worked at companies like Jupiter Money, Youplus, Apigee (now Google) and Cisco. Most recently, I led the org wide Applied AI effort as a Principal Applied Scientist at Jupiter Money for multiple charters including customer service, personal finance management and recommendations & personalization. I have a Master's degree from King's College London in Intelligent Systems and an undergraduate degree from Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani in Information Systems. I'm a published researcher and have coauthored papers at tier-1 conferences like COLING 2020 (BiomedBERT) and WWW 2018 (Illuminating the Ecosystem of Partisan Websites) which have been collectively cited 100+ times. I actively contributes to the AI/ML community through technical talks, workshops and blogs that have been published in KDNuggets, TowardsDataScience and Google's Dev Library. Along with that I'm recognized as a Google Developer Expert in AI & Cloud and a Google Cloud Champion Innovator.
