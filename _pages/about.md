I'm Shweta, an Applied Scientist and an AI advisor. Over the past decade, I've led applied AI across fintech, e-commerce, and biomedical domains, designing and shipping systems that span conversational agents, personalization engines, intelligent search, and LLM-powered reasoning pipelines. Most recently, I led Applied Science at [Jupiter Money](https://jupiter.money/), where I  architected conversational AI and PFM products for a 3M+ user base, delivering $3M in annual cost reductions. I'm recognized as [Google Developer Expert (GDE)](https://developers.google.com/community/experts) in AI and have mentored startups on AI strategy and implementation through the Google for Startups Accelerator. I'm frequent speaker at various tech meetups and events and an active contributor to the global AI/ML community. I graduated from [King’s College London](https://www.kcl.ac.uk/) with a Master's in Artificial Intelligence. I studied Information Systems at [BITS Pilani](https://www.bits-pilani.ac.in/).
