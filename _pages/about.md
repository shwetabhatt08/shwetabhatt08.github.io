I'm frequently speak at various tech meetups and events and actively contribute to the global AI/ML community. I graduated from [King’s College London](https://www.kcl.ac.uk/) with a Master's in Artificial Intelligence. I studied Information Systems at [BITS Pilani](https://www.bits-pilani.ac.in/).
