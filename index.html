Biography

I am a Data Scientist based out of Bangalore, India. Currently working at Youplus where I focus on building products using Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing. My primary interests include NLP, Deep Learning and Reinforcement Learning.

I am a Google Developer Expert(GDE) in Machine Learning since August 2019 and also an AI/ML Mentor at Springboard .

I did my postgraduate degree in Artificial Intelligence at King's College London in 2017 and my undergraduate degree in Information Systems at Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani in 2012.
